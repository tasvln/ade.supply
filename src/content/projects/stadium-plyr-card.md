## Overview

The Stadium Player Card system is a dynamic solution for generating unique SVG-based cards for players and teams participating in tournaments. Each card encapsulates vital statistics and performance metrics, providing a comprehensive overview of a player's or team's worth for recruitment and ownership purposes. This system is designed to enhance the gaming experience by offering a collectible aspect similar to traditional trading cards, but tailored for the esports community â€” think of it as a pokemon card but for gamers.

![Final UI](/images/projects/stadium/player-card-cover.png)

## Features

- **Dynamic SVG Generation**: Each player or team card is generated as an SVG, allowing for high-quality visuals that can be easily scaled and customized.
- **Comprehensive Stats Integration**: Cards display a wide range of statistics, including performance metrics, win rates, and other relevant data points that reflect the player's or team's abilities.
- **Interactive Elements**: Cards can include interactive elements such as hover effects and clickable areas that provide additional information or links to player profiles.
- **Customizable Designs**: Users can choose from various card designs and themes to personalize their cards.
- **Easy Sharing and Exporting**: Cards can be easily shared on social media or exported for printing, making them perfect for both digital and physical collections.
- **Backend Integration**: The system integrates with existing tournament databases to automatically update player and team stats in real-time.
